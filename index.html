Mapping Colombian Emblematic Memories

Mapping Colombian Emblematic Memories is an ongoing project seeking to produce a visual representation of the linkage between emblematic frameworks of meaning about the past and place.

The emblematic memories the project is mapping are related to the Colombian inner armed conflict's legacies and traumas. The map contains a growing amount of sites of memory different social movements and institutions of the state are putting in place as singular elements connected to more extensive infrastructures of remembrance.

Mapping Colombian Emblematic Memories is not a project seeking to present a harmonic picture about the past. The project makes visible the struggles for recognition that hundreds of thousands of survivors are giving against different forms of oblivion. At the same time, it draws the contentious and embodied character of summoning the past. Hopefully, mapping the plurality of emblematic memories about the past will enable productive discussions and meaningful solidarities to find historical alternatives to the Colombian specters of violence and exclusion.
