Mapping Emblematic Memories is a digital humanities project mapping various Colombian sites of memory related to the inner armed conflict. It also provides conceptual definitions to navigate the Colombian politics of memory. It uses and facilitates public access information of different and sometimes conflicting stakeholders trying to have a say in public debates about the Colombian past.

...

x

The project's objective is to visulize the linkage between space and memory, and to serve as a plataform to critically think about the Colombian politics of memory. Still, it is also aiming to create a unified database of the Colombian sites of memory related to the Colombian armed conflict. Such a data base should be useful for scholars and activists looking to build peace in Colombia.
