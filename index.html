Mapping Colombian Emblematic Memories

Mapping Colombian Emblematic Memories is an ongoing project seeking to produce a visual representation of the linkage between popular and powerful depictions of the past and place.

The emblematic memories the project is mapping are related to the Colombian inner armed conflict's legacies, traumas, and embodied intepretations. The map contains a growing amount of sites of memory different social movements and institutions of the state are putting in place. Those physical places have been starting to be enunciated by different stakeholders as more extensive networks we will call infrastructures of remembrance.

Mapping Colombian Emblematic Memories is not a project seeking to present a harmonic picture about the past. The project makes visible the struggles for recognition that various and sometimes conflicting groups are giving against different forms of oblivion. At the same time, it draws the contentious and embodied character of summoning the past. Hopefully, mapping the plurality of emblematic memories about the past in Colombia will help to produce meaningful discussions and solidarities to find alternatives to the Colombian specters of violence and exclusion.
